Welcome to Yaddah

Explore the Democratic Republic of Yaddah, a unique micronation dedicated to preserving democracy and cultural heritage. Located in Central Israel, Yaddah was founded as a response to local concerns about preserving democratic values and cultural identity.

About Us

Founded on April 5, 2023, by Itay Dayan, Yaddah emerged as a peaceful symbol of democracy. With a focus on Jewish values and traditions, Yaddah promotes a democratic culture that prioritizes transparency and equality.

History

The first elections in Yaddah saw Itay Dayan’s defeat to Banya Dayan, but irregularities were soon uncovered, leading to a new election. This incident has since influenced Yaddah's commitment to electoral integrity and transparency.

Government

Yaddah’s government is a democratic republic, with regular elections to ensure fair representation. The nation’s dedication to democratic values is reflected in its transparent election processes and leadership commitments.

Culture

As a Jewish micronation, Yaddah celebrates traditions that honor Jewish heritage. Community events and national holidays offer residents the chance to engage with their history and culture.
