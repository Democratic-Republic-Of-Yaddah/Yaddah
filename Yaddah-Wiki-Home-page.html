Featured Article

The Democratic Republic of Yaddah is a self-proclaimed micronation located in Gedera, Israel. It was founded on April 5, 2023, by Itay Dayan with the goal of creating a society that emphasizes democratic principles, Jewish cultural heritage, and transparency in governance. Yaddah serves as a peaceful symbol for promoting democratic values while preserving the cultural identity of the Jewish people.

Did You Know?

Yaddahism is a political ideology developed by Itay Dayan from Israel. The ideology advocates for a combination of modern liberal principles, Zionism, and capitalism, while maintaining key public institutions under state ownership and partial regulation of certain products. Yaddahism views Zionism as a unifying factor but does not include racist views toward other population groups. It opposes the two-state solution to the Israeli-Palestinian conflict, believing that more aggressive actions are the way to advance security and national goals.
